About Us

Where English Becomes an Adventure

At Anh Ngu Fun English, we believe learning a language should feel like play, not work. Founded right here in our community, our school was built on a single idea: every student learns better when they feel safe, encouraged, and genuinely having fun.

Our curriculum blends games, storytelling, and real-world conversations to build vocabulary, grammar, and confidence — at exactly the right pace for each learner.
